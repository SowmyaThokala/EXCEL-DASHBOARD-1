## Project Overview

This Excel-based analysis provides insights into business performance across various departments. The dataset includes financial and operational information such as revenue, profit, expenses, payment methods, product lines, and regional transactions.

The project leverages Pivot Tables to summarize and visualize the company’s key performance indicators (KPIs), enabling decision-makers to quickly identify profitable areas, cost centers, and sales trends.

## Dataset Description

The imported dataset contains the following key columns:

Department – Business unit or functional area

Revenue – Total income generated by each department

Expenses – Total costs incurred

Profit – Net earnings (Revenue – Expenses)

Payment Method – Mode of transaction (e.g., Cash, UPI, Credit Card, etc.)

Product Line – Category of products sold

Region – Geographical location of transactions

Transaction ID – Unique identifier for each sale

## Pivot Table Analysis
## Pivot 1: Profit by Payment Method

Rows: Payment Method

Values: Sum of Profit

Insight: Identifies which payment modes contribute most to overall profitability.

## Pivot 2: Revenue by Product Line

Rows: Product Line

Values: Sum of Revenue

Insight: Helps in understanding which product lines generate the highest sales revenue.

## Pivot 3: Departmental Financial Summary

Rows: Department

Values:

Sum of Revenue

Sum of Expenses

Sum of Profit

Insight: Provides a consolidated view of departmental performance and cost efficiency.

## Pivot 4: Regional Transaction Count

Rows: Region

Values: Count of Transaction ID

Insight: Reveals transaction volume across different regions to assess market activity.

## Pivot 5: Average Expenses by Department

Rows: Department

Values: Average of Expenses

Insight: Helps in identifying cost management efficiency across departments.
